## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of MarginNote for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move MarginNote.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.

## Overview

<a href="#">MarginNote for macOS</a> is an innovative note-taking app specifically designed for students, researchers, and anyone who needs to organize and interact with information in a deeply engaging way. It goes beyond just taking notes by offering a variety of powerful tools that help you highlight, annotate, and connect information from various sources. Whether you're studying textbooks, PDFs, or web pages, MarginNote allows you to consolidate your learning and make connections between concepts effortlessly.

The appâ€™s unique feature is its ability to build mind maps and outlines directly from your notes, turning fragmented information into cohesive knowledge structures. These visual elements help you understand and memorize complex topics. MarginNote supports annotations with highlights, notes, and flashcards, providing a comprehensive study toolset. You can also sync your notes and flashcards with Anki, making it even easier to retain information for exams.

The integration with various file formats like PDFs, ePub, and web pages means you can import all your study materials into MarginNote, where you can annotate and organize them in one place. With its powerful search functionality and tag system, MarginNote makes it simple to find specific content and review important concepts whenever you need them.

## Major Features
- Mind map creation directly from notes for a visual overview  
- Annotate PDFs, ePub files, and web pages  
- Flashcard creation and integration with Anki  
- Deep integration of note-taking with outlining and mind mapping  
- Advanced search and tagging for easy navigation  
- PDF highlight, comment, and bookmark tools  
- Synchronized reading progress across devices  
- Handwriting and digital note support  
- Visual note editing to improve learning and retention  
- High-quality PDF export options for presentation or sharing  
- Text-to-speech support for audio-based learning  
- Smart outlines to organize content for review
