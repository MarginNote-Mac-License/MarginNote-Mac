## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of Freedom for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move Freedom.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.

## Overview


        <a href="#">Freedom for macOS</a> is a powerful app that helps you block distractions, allowing you to stay focused on what matters most. Whether you're working on a project, writing, or studying, Freedom blocks distracting websites and apps, helping you stay productive. The app lets you customize your distractions by blocking specific websites or applications, ensuring that you stay focused on your tasks.

        The app offers scheduling features that allow you to set up blocks of time where you can work uninterrupted. You can also create recurring sessions, so Freedom becomes an essential part of your daily routine. Freedom syncs across all your devices, ensuring that you're not distracted whether you're working on your Mac or other platforms.

        Freedom integrates smoothly with macOS, giving you full control over your digital distractions. Whether you're trying to beat procrastination or simply need a little help focusing, Freedom ensures that you stay on track and achieve your goals faster by blocking time-wasting apps and websites.

## Major Features
- Block distracting websites and apps to maintain focus  
- Create custom blocklists to prevent access to specific content  
- Schedule focus sessions to block distractions at set times  
- Sync across devices to block distractions anywhere  
- Track your progress and productivity with session reports  
- Work offline with full blocking functionality without the internet  
- App and website usage analysis to identify digital habits  
- Break the habit of browsing social media or unnecessary sites
